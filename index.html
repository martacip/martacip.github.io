About me

Hi! Welcome to my webpage.
I am Marta Cipriani, a PhD student in Methodological Statistics at Sapienza University of Rome.

My research focuses on survival analysis, with particular interest in:

  • mixture cure models
  • dynamic prediction and landmarking
  • multiple imputation for missing data
  • synthetic data generation for clinical trials

Previously, I worked as a research fellow at Roma Tre University and was a visiting PhD student at the Mathematical Institute of Leiden University. I have been involved in research projects on clinical trial methodology and real-world data analysis, in collaboration with the GIMEMA Foundation.

Starting in February 2026, I will join the School of Mathematics and Statistics at University College Dublin as a postdoctoral researcher.

If you would like to know more, you can find my CV here.
