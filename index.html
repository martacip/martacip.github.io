Marta Cipriani

Hi! I am Marta Cipriani, a PhD student in Methodological Statistics at Sapienza University of Rome. My research focuses on survival analysis, with particular interest in:

  • Mixture cure models
  • Dynamic prediction and landmarking
  • Multiple imputation for missing data
  • Synthetic data generation for clinical trials

Previously, I worked as a research fellow at the University of Roma Tre, and collaborated on projects in clinical trial methodology with GIMEMA Foundation and the Mathematical Institute at Leiden University.
